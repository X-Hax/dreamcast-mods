Entering CamEdit does not get rid of the player and the player can still perform a spindash while the editor initialises.

Y+X: Activate CamEdit
A+B: Toggle Mode (Set/System)

SET:
X: X Axis Adjustment
Y: Y Axis Adjustment
B: Z Axis Adjustment
A+U/D - Edit Element
A+L/R - Camera Mode
A+X - Camera Level
Start+A: Grab Camera
Start+B: Place Camera
Start+X: Adjust Mode
Start+Y: Collision Type


SYSTEM:
B: Toggle visible cameras
Y: Toggle visible display (Full/Minimal/Hidden)
A+X: Exit CamEdit
